This is a memory game based on a webpage. Player moves the mouse onto the card and the card will be reversed, and player moves the mouse onto another card. If the numbers on the two cards are equal, the cards will not reverse back when the player moves the mouse out of the card. If the numbers on the two cards are not equal, two cards will reversed back and the numbers on the cards will disappear. Game over when player reversed all the card successfully. An attempt to reverse two cards is called one "move". Player will get 3 stars if the game is finished within 10 moves, 2 stars if the game is finished between 11 and 13 moves and 1 star if the game is finished in more than 13 moves. Time spent is recorded.
